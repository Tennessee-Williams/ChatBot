ACME Logistics Inc. — Leave of Absence Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

ACME provides the following leave types:
- FMLA: Up to 12 weeks unpaid leave for qualifying medical or family reasons.
- Parental Leave: 6 weeks paid leave for birth, adoption, or foster placement.
- Bereavement: 3 days paid leave for immediate family; 1 day for extended family.
- Military Leave: In accordance with USERRA regulations.

Employees must notify HR at least 30 days in advance for foreseeable leave. Medical certification may be required.
