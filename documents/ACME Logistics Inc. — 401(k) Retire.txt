ACME Logistics Inc. — 401(k) Retirement Plan
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Employees are eligible to participate in the 401(k) plan after 90 days of employment. ACME matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary. Contributions are pre-tax and reduce taxable income. Employees are immediately vested in their own contributions; company match vests over 3 years (33% per year). The annual IRS contribution limit for 2025 is $23,000 ($30,500 for employees age 50+).
