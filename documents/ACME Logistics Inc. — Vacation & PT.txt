ACME Logistics Inc. — Vacation & PTO Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Full-time employees accrue PTO at the following rates:
- 0–2 years of service: 10 days per year
- 3–5 years of service: 15 days per year
- 6+ years of service: 20 days per year

PTO requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance. A maximum of 5 unused PTO days may be rolled over to the following year. PTO is not paid out upon resignation unless required by state law.
