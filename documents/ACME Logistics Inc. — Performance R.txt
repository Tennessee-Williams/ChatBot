ACME Logistics Inc. — Performance Review Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

All full-time employees receive a formal performance review annually in December. New employees receive a 90-day review. Reviews are conducted by the direct manager and include self-evaluation. Performance ratings are: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement, and Unsatisfactory. Merit increases are tied to performance ratings and distributed in January. Employees rated Needs Improvement will be placed on a performance improvement plan (PIP).
