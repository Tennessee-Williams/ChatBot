ACME Logistics Inc. — Expense Reimbursement Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Employees will be reimbursed for pre-approved business expenses. Mileage reimbursement is at the current IRS standard rate ($0.67/mile in 2025). Meals during business travel are reimbursed up to $75/day. All expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of incurring the expense with original receipts. Personal expenses will not be reimbursed.
